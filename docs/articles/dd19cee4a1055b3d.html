Overview

Consistent image builds, or golden images are an important part of any standard operating environment (SOE). You need to guarantee that the software in the image remains consistent from build to build until you update your approved package set. Beyond the image, you need to be sure that your running systems are receiving the same updates as your image, so that you can maintain consistency throughout your environment.
