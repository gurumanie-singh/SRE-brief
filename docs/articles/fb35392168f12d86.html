Root cause highlight

Likely root-cause theme: control plane issue. Confidential Computing GPUs extend the CPU's trusted execution environment to the GPU and open the door to many use cases where using a shared infrastructure (cloud, colocation, edge) was not possible in the past due to the need to protect data and code in use, and because previous confidential computing solutions were not performant or flexible enough for many workloads. Discover NVIDIA GPUs - including their capabilities - and advertise them to the OpenShift control plane.
