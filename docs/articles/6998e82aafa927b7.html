Lessons learned

Building an automatic evaluation system that works reliably in practice is hard, and the approach we have described reflects countless lessons learned through iteration to improve accuracy and scalability.

To reach a better consensus, we conducted calibration rounds (~50 synopses per round), surfaced disagreements, and evolved our quality scoring guidelines.
