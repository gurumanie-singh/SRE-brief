Vera: A Layered Video Diffusion Model Existing video editing models regenerate the entire clip, coupling the intended edit with regions that should remain unchanged.

By design, Vera supports complex tasks such as object addition and background change, while ensuring that the pixels outside the edited regions from the source video remain perfectly intact.

Model Training During model training for VOID, we introduce two improvements over prior work: (i) quadmask conditioning, which explicitly identifies regions in each frame that may change after the object is removed, and (ii) a second-pass video appearance refiner that reduces artifacts such as unwanted object morphing.

Evaluations and Results Experiments across both synthetic and real data demonstrate that VOID preserves consistent scene dynamics far better than prior video object removal methods (please see the paper for full results).
