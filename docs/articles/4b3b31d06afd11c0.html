Resolution / fix

AMD uses its own prefix conventions.

Instead, the system queries Prometheus at startup for metrics that match configurable vendor prefixes.

To prepare for new metric naming conventions, platform teams can supply additional prefixes via environment variables: GPU_METRICS_PREFIX_NVIDIA , GPU_METRICS_PREFIX_INTEL , and GPU_METRICS_PREFIX_AMD .

Custom prefixes extend the built-in defaults instead of replacing them.
