Overview

Organizations are rapidly building AI applications, deploying AI agents, and scaling Azure environments faster than ever before. As AI initiatives accelerate and the volume of code expands, many organizations discover they have lost visibility into the infrastructure supporting those workloads. Resources created outside standard workflows, unmanaged environments, and infrastructure drift create governance, security, and operational challenges that become increasingly difficult to control at scale.
